I am passionate about genealogy. This website is planned to contain a copy of my family tree in the future.

My ancestry consists of:

  • Czech branches from multiple areas of Central Bohemia, each including its vicinity:
    • Příbram,
    • Kozárovice, in the area between Příbram and Písek,
    • Rožmitál pod Třemšínem,
    • Hořovice and nearby Hvozdec and Strašice,
    • Červený Hrádek near Kolín,
    some ancestors from these branches have arrived in the area of Most (where I would later grow up) around the year 1900 for work in coal mines and settled there,
  • a German branch from Northern Bohemia, specifically the area of Šluknov, with arrivals from multiple places in Germany in the distant past,
  • a Moravian branch from Topolná near Uherské Hradiště.

Important sources: church and civil records, as well as census operates, mostly from today's Czech Republic. Digitized records available through regional archives, see link.
